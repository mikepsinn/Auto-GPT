- Open a terminal or Git Bash\n- Type 'ssh-keygen -t rsa -b 4096 -C 'your_email@example.com'' and press enter\n- Press enter again to save the key in the default location\n- Enter a passphrase (optional)\n- Go to your GitHub settings page and click on 'SSH and GPG keys'\n- Click on 'New SSH key'\n- Give your key a title and paste the contents of your public key into the 'key' input box\n- Click on 'Add SSH key' to save your key
